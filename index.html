On Saturday, June 27th, the Pitser Garrison Convention Center transforms into the ultimate destination for anyone who takes pride in where they live. Homeowners, renters, DIY enthusiasts, gardeners, weekend warriors, first-time buyers...if you've ever had a project on your list, this is your day.

Walk the floor and meet the local businesses, makers, and experts who can help you get it done. Get real quotes. See live demos. Discover products and services you didn't know existed right here in your backyard. And don't leave without entering one of our giveaways.
